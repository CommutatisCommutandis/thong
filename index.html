THONG

A Quick Look plugin to view your .string files with a simple touch.

Download »

Why

I was really tired to always have to open my Vim editor to quickly view the content of my .strings file or any file without a file extension.

Just looking at it without doing some modifications, it just was too much clicking. So I decided to create my own super awesome Quick Look plugin.
